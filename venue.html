Melbourne Cricket Ground

The Melbourne Cricket Ground (MCG), also known locally as Australian sports stadium located in Yarra Park, Melbourne, Victoria. Founded and managed by the Melbourne Cricket Club, it is the largest stadium in the Southern Hemisphere, the 11th largest globally, and the second largest cricket ground by capacity.

Eden Gardens

Eden Gardens is a cricket ground in Kolkata, India. Established in 1864, it is the oldest[3][4][5] and second-largest cricket stadium in India after the newly built Narendra Modi Stadium and third-largest in the world after Narendra Modi Stadium and the Melbourne Cricket Ground. The stadium currently has a capacity of 68,000.

Narendra Modi Stadium

The Narendra Modi Stadium, formerly known as the Motera Stadium, is a cricket stadium situated inside the Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel Sports Enclave in Ahmedabad, Gujarat, India. As of 2022, it is the largest stadium in the world, with a seating capacity of 132,000 spectators
